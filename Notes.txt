 What is Frontend?
 Frontend is what we see on a website.

EX: When you open a website like Instagram — the buttons, colors, text, and layout — all that is frontend.


 What is Backend?
Backend is the part of a website that works in the background and manages data.”

EX: When you log in to Instagram:”

You enter your username and password (Frontend)

That info is sent to the backend, which checks if it’s correct or not


Database: A database is a place where data is stored and managed.
🔗 Connection Between Frontend, Backend, and Database
📱 Frontend
The part you see and use — like Instagram’s login page, feed, like button, etc.
🗣 “When you type your username and password and click login.”

⚙️ Backend
The part that receives your request, checks if your info is correct, and decides what to do.
🗣 “It checks your login details.”

🗃 Database
The place where all your data is stored — like usernames, passwords, posts, followers.
🗣 “It finds your details in the database and sends them back to the backend.”'
